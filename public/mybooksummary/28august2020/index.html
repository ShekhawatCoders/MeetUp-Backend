Follow these Seven steps :

  • First, decide exactly what you want in each area of your life , especially in your financial life.
  • Second, write down your goals clearly and specifically
  • Third, set a deadline for each goal.set subdead-line if a goal is big enough.
  • Fourth, make a list of everything you will have to do achieve each goal.
  • Fift, organise your list into a plan of action.Determine what you are going to do first and what you will do later.Decide what is more important and what is less important.
  • Sixth, take action on your plan immediately(my favourite one)
  • Seventh, do something everyday that moves you at least one step closer to your most important goal.
