Side Hustle

1. The Way OF The Hustle
  • Everyone should have side hustle.Even if you love your job, having more than one source of income will give you more freedom and more options.
  • Side hustles are all about action!You need to launch your idea, usually before you feel totally ready,then regroup and refine after seeing the initial results.
  • Sure for some people the thought of quitting their day job and striking out on their own is exhilarating.For many others, however it can br terrifying.
  • Side hustle is new job security.
  • More income means more options , More options mean more freedom.
  • Money does grow on trees - you just have to plant the right seeds,in the right soil.
2. Predict The Future
  • GOAL #1 : Make some extra cash for a specific purpose, whether to pay off a loan, buy a big-ticket item, take a vacation, or just build up your emergency fund.
  • GOAL #2 : Create a sustainable and ongoing source of income that makes a real difference in your quality of life.
  • GOAL #3 : Replace or exceed the income form your current job.
  • A side hustle is like a hobby, with one big difference : most hobbies cost money and A side hustle makes money.
  • It's like getting paid to try something new and learn different skills.
3. Learn How Money grows on Trees
  • For your tree to bear fruit, you'll need to put int the work to turn those ideas into action.
  • But your first step is to find the right ideas.
  • Every hustle start with an idea - and for best results, you may need more than one.
  • You want your idea to be feasible,profitableandpersuasive.

    • Is Your Idea Feasible ?

    • Start a Project / That earns money / In a short period of time.
    • A feasible idea is one that you can turn into reality using the skills, time, and resources you already have.

    Is Your Idea Profitable ?

    • If you don't see how you could make money from the idea, preferably in a short amount of time, it's probably not a good idea.

    Is Your Idea Pursuasive ?

    • To be successful, you want the right idea at the right time.

    High-potential ideas have characteristics like these :

    • A simple path to turn the idea into reality that you can desire in one sentence.
    • Something that you know how to do or can easily figure out.
    • Solves a problem or makes someone's life easier in a specific way(and that they will be willing to pay for).
    • Is low maintenance and easy to deliver without a ton of preparation or follow-up.
    • Will bring in income not just once but on a recurring basis.
